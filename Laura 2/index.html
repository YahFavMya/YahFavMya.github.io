All About
Mya Bryant

Over my years of experience with photography, I’ve learned a lot of fun, new interesting facts that I didn’t know about. So today I would like to show you the experience I’ve had to maybe influence/persuade you to reach for what you love and never give up.

 

I’ve started photography since I was 13 years old. I’d love taking pictures of places and I would always hope that someone would recognize my work and purchase them. No one did. So I decided to work harder and go to places where I knew I would get noticed. I took pictures at the beach, of nature, of buildings, and even animal life. I was inspired to do photography from my uncle. He's a professional photographer and I just love what he does. I hope you enjoy my pictures. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions.
